About

A former Army Ranger and lifelong techie, I revel in building software that simplifies and delights our everyday lives. The son of an artist and a psychologist, I’m both creative and analytical.

At age ten, I keypunched a tic-tac-toe game in Fortran. That led to learning AppleSoft BASIC and other compiled languages. I’m also as much a designer as I am a developer, fluent in Apple Human Interface Guidelines. I meticulously craft every UI pixel of my work.

I’m a proud spouse, parent of three, and grandparent of two. When not coding, I enjoy live music and Crimson Tide Football.
