About

More About Me.

Initially self-taught, I began mastering Cocoa and Objective-C almost a decade ago by contributing to an open-source project. When iOS emerged, I was fortunate to earn a living from this know-how.

Developing exclusively on the Apple Platform has given me a deep understanding of its framework conventions, memory management, tools, and best practices. I’m passionate about my code’s quality, architecture, and maintainability. As much a graphic designer as a developer, I craft every single UI pixel.

I’m a proud husband, father of three, and grandfather of one. When not coding, I wrangle our spirited Weimaraner, enjoy live music, and Crimson Tide football.
