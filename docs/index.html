About

From Camouflage Back to Coding

As the child of an artist and a psychologist, I’m both creative and analytical. At age ten, I keypunched a tic-tac-toe game in Fortran. That led to learning AppleSoft BASIC and other compiled languages.

A former Army Ranger and lifelong techie, I returned to programming in 2015, bringing qualities like adaptability, resolve, insight, and systematic thinking to the table. These dovetail perfectly with software development.

I’m also a UI/UX designer—fluent in Apple Human Interface Guidelines—who meticulously crafts every pixel of my work. Building software that simplifies and delights our everyday lives is my deep-rooted passion.

I’m a proud spouse, parent of three, and grandparent of two. When not coding, I enjoy wrangling our Weimaraner, live music, and Crimson Tide Football.
