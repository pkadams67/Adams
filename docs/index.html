Hello, I’m Paul

I’m a Nashville area software developer who creates apps and practical solutions for the Apple platform.

About

As the son of an artist and a psychologist, I’m both creative and analytical. My start in programming began as a preteen, coding a tic-tac-toe game in Fortran. That led to learning AppleSoft BASIC and other compiled languages, beginning an enduring passion for writing software that simplifies and delights our everyday lives.

Work

I mostly program in Swift, coding as needed in other languages such as Objective-C, Ruby, and HTML/CSS/JavaScript. I’ve had the honor of working with several class-leading software companies, collaborating on over 200 titles among a dozen different App Store categories. It’s a remarkable feeling knowing my code has been in the hands of more than a million users.
