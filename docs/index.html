About

Glad to meet you!

I started learning Cocoa and Objective‑C almost a decade ago by contributing to an open-source project. When Cocoa Touch and Swift emerged, I was fortunate to earn a living from iOS Development.

Working exclusively on the Apple Platform has given me a deep understanding of its framework conventions, memory management, tools, and best practices. I’m passionate about my code’s quality, architecture, and maintainability. As much a graphic designer as a developer, I craft every pixel.

I’m a proud spouse, parent of three, and grandparent of one. When not coding, I wrangle our spirited Weimaraner, enjoy live music, and Crimson Tide Football.
