Pleased to Meet You!

As the son of an artist and a psychologist, I’m both creative and analytical. My start in programming began as a preteen, coding a tic-tac-toe game in Fortran. That led to learning AppleSoft BASIC and other compiled languages, starting an enduring passion for writing software that simplifies and delights our everyday lives.

Today, I mostly work in Swift, Apple’s modern programming language. I’ve had the honor of working with several class-leading software companies, collaborating on over 200 titles among a dozen different App Store categories. It’s a remarkable feeling knowing my code has been in the hands of more than a million users.
